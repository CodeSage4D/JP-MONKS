Flagship Platform

MonkAcademy ERP

A comprehensive multi-campus dashboard engine built for modern academic institutions. MonkAcademy integrates student lifecycles, academic grade scaling, and online fee collection systems into a singular, highly isolated PostgreSQL database schema.

  • **Unified Campus Rosters**: Manage admissions pipelines, registration cards, and transcript archives smoothly.
  • **Smart Billing Ledgers**: Fully automated fee schedule triggers and secure online banking gateways.
  • **Advanced Gradebooks**: Modular grading frameworks supporting custom school credit rules.

MonkExpense Engine

A private-first personal finance tracker and automated ledger. We are deploying native wrappers to release this natively on the **Google Play Store**.

  • **Local Invoice Parse**: Highly optimized scanner processes data entirely within your smartphone.
  • **Instant Offline Mode**: Log cash transaction cards off-grid; auto-syncs securely when connected.
